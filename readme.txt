== Description ==

LianaMailer for Contact Form 7 is an integration plugin which integrates LianaMailer into [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/). It enables you to have newsletter opt-ins automatically exported from Contact Form 7 form to the chosen mailing list in LianaMailer email marketing solution.

= Collect contacts easily from your WordPress site =

LianaMailer allows you to segment contacts, collect email contact lists, and send device-optimized messages in line with your brand with just a few clicks. With this tool, you can develop your performance based on real data and comprehensive reports.

Save time and reduce manual work by using LianaMailer for Contact Form 7 to collect contacts, such as newsletter and guide subscribers, from your WordPress site.
